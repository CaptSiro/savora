# Grilled Chicken: Juicy Perfection with Simple Herbs and Light Seasoning

Grilled chicken is a staple that never goes out of style. Known for its juicy goodness and versatile flavor, this dish becomes even more delightful when prepared with simple herbs and light seasoning. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue or whipping up a quick weeknight dinner, grilled chicken can be both satisfying and uncomplicated. 

## The Beauty of Simplicity

When it comes to grilled chicken, less truly is more. Using uncomplicated herbs allows the natural flavor of the chicken to shine through. Here are some tips to elevate your chicken with just a few ingredients:

### Key Ingredients  

- **Chicken breasts or thighs:** Chicken thighs are juicier, while breasts are leaner. Choose based on your preference. 

- **Olive oil:** A drizzle helps keep the chicken moist and allows the seasoning to stick. 

- **Herbs:** Fresh or dried herbs like rosemary, thyme, or basil can add a burst of flavor without overwhelming the dish. 

- **Salt and pepper:** Essential for bringing out the natural taste of the meat. 

- **Lemon juice:** A splash adds brightness and pairs beautifully with chicken.  

## Preparing Your Chicken 

### Step-by-Step Guide  

#### Marinate:  

- Combine olive oil, your chosen herbs, salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice in a bowl.  

- Add the chicken pieces, coating them well. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes (or longer if you have time!).  

#### Preheat the Grill:

- Get your grill nice and hot. If you’re using charcoal, let the coals ash over. For gas grills, preheat to medium-high.

#### Grill It Up:  

- Place the marinated chicken on the grill. Make sure not to crowd the grill; give each piece room to breathe.  

- Grill for about 6–7 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F. 

#### Rest and Serve:

- Remove the chicken from the grill, let it rest for about 5 minutes, then slice and serve. This helps keep it juicy!

## Serving Suggestions 

Grilled chicken is incredibly versatile. Here are a few simple serving ideas to make your meal complete:

- **With a Salad:** Serve it over a bed of greens with your favorite vinaigrette. 

- **On a Sandwich:** Layer sliced grilled chicken with avocado, lettuce, and a light spread of mayo or mustard. 

- **With Grilled Veggies:** Pair your chicken with seasonal grilled vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, or corn.

## Final Thoughts  

Grilled chicken doesn’t have to be complicated to be delicious. With just a handful of simple herbs and light seasoning, you can create a dish that’s not only juicy and flavorful but also easy to prepare. So fire up the grill, embrace the simplicity, and enjoy a delicious meal that the whole family will love! 

Happy grilling!
